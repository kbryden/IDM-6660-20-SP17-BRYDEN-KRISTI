Welcome to Pete's restaurants. We have been a Denver tradition for 50 years. We are family owned and operated beginning at the Satire Lounge. We have expanded from there and now have six great locations. We specialize in terrific home cooked meals with great service. We have a location for whatever your looking for. Breakfast, lunch, dinner, private parties or catering (American-Greek or just traditional authentic Greek food). Please stop in one of our restaurants and we will help you experience Pete's!
